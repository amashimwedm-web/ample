Our History

Our Story (About ABM)

American-Burundi Mall (ABM) was created to bring together modern shopping and local culture in one unique destination. Inspired by the strong partnership between Burundi and the United States, ABM was designed as a space where people can shop, relax, and connect. The mall brings international standards of retail, dining, and entertainment while celebrating Burundian identity, creativity, and hospitality. ABM is more than a shopping center; it is a place where families, friends, and communities come together to enjoy quality services in a safe and welcoming environment.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a modern, accessible, and customer-focused shopping experience that meets international standards while supporting local businesses and communities. American-Burundi Mall aims to offer diverse products and services, excellent customer care, and a comfortable environment where every visitor feels valued, respected, and satisfied.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading shopping and lifestyle mall in the region, recognized for innovation, cultural integration, and excellence in service delivery. We aim to make American-Burundi Mall a symbol of development, partnership, and opportunity, contributing positively to economic growth and social interaction in Burundi and beyond.
